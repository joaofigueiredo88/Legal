Privacy Policy

Effective: February 13, 2026 · Tasso Software Studio · Switzerland

1. Who We Are

Kypo is developed by Tasso Software Studio, based in Switzerland. For privacy inquiries, contact us at tassosoftwarestudio@gmail.com.

2. Zero-Knowledge Architecture

Kypo cannot read your data. All family content (notes, tasks, calendar events, budgets, shopping lists) is encrypted locally on your device using AES-256-GCM before it ever leaves the device. Encryption keys are derived from credentials that Tasso Software Studio never receives, stores, or can reconstruct.

This means:

  • We hold zero encryption keys.
  • We have zero access to the content of your V2 Vault.
  • Even if compelled by law, we cannot produce your decrypted data because we do not possess the means to decrypt it.

3. What Data We Process

We do not collect analytics, advertising identifiers, location data, or contact lists.

4. Cloud Anchor — Your Storage, Your Choice

Kypo syncs your encrypted vault to a Cloud Anchor that you select:

  • Google Drive (Google LLC) — Privacy Policy
  • OneDrive (Microsoft Corporation) — Privacy Policy
  • iCloud (Apple Inc.) — Privacy Policy

Kypo stores the encrypted blob in the provider's app-specific hidden folder. The cloud provider can see the blob but cannot decrypt its contents. Their respective privacy policies govern the storage layer.

5. Legal Basis for Processing

Under the Swiss Federal Act on Data Protection (nFADP) and the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Contract performance (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR): Processing your account data to provide the service you requested.
  • Legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR): Anti-abuse measures and service stability.
  • Consent: Push notifications are opt-in.

6. Data Transfers

Your encrypted vault may be stored by Google, Microsoft, or Apple in data centres outside Switzerland and the EU. Switzerland is recognised by the EU as providing adequate data protection. For transfers to the US, these providers participate in the EU-US Data Privacy Framework.

7. Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained while your account exists. Deleted within 30 days of account deletion.
  • Encrypted vault: Stored on your Cloud Anchor under your control. Kypo does not retain a copy.
  • Legacy data: After migration from the legacy FamilyOrganizer app, legacy collections are permanently archived and blocked.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data.
  • Rectify inaccurate data.
  • Erase your data (“right to be forgotten”).
  • Port your data to another service.
  • Object to processing based on legitimate interest.
  • Lodge a complaint with the Swiss Federal Data Protection and Information Commissioner (FDPIC) or your local EU supervisory authority.

To exercise any right, email tassosoftwarestudio@gmail.com.

9. Children

Kypo is intended for users aged 16 or older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 16. If you believe a child under 16 has created an account, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

10. Security

Beyond zero-knowledge encryption, we employ TLS for all network communication, Firestore security rules restricting data access to authenticated family members, and transaction-safe migration locking to prevent data races.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in the law or our practices. Material changes will be communicated via an in-app notice at least 14 days before they take effect. The “Effective” date at the top indicates the current version.

1. Acceptance

By creating an account or using Kypo, you agree to these Terms and our Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, do not use the service. You must be at least 16 years old to use Kypo.

2. Description of Service

Kypo is a household management application that provides shared notes, tasks, calendar events, shopping lists, budgets, and expense group tracking for families and groups. All family data is encrypted locally and synced to a user-selected Cloud Anchor.

3. Roles & Responsibilities

3.1 Founder

The Founder is the family member who creates a family and establishes its Cloud Anchor. The Founder is responsible for:

  • Linking a cloud storage provider (Google Drive, OneDrive, or iCloud) to serve as the family’s Cloud Anchor.
  • Initiating and overseeing the data migration from the legacy FamilyOrganizer app.
  • Managing family membership (inviting and removing members).
  • Maintaining the encryption key chain for the family. If the Founder loses access to their account, encrypted data may become unrecoverable.

3.2 Members

Members are invited by the Founder and receive encrypted access to shared family data. Members can create and edit shared content according to the role assigned by the Founder (admin, edit, or view).

4.1 Member Expansion Slots

Additional member slots may be purchased for €1.00 per slot per family or expense group. Each purchase permanently increases the member cap by one for that specific family or group. Expansion slot purchases are non-refundable.

5. Legacy Migration & Sunset

5.1 Migration from FamilyOrganizer

Kypo provides a one-time migration path from the legacy FamilyOrganizer app (Firebase project: familyorganizer-a5a3c). The migration extracts, transforms, encrypts, and injects legacy data into the local V2 Vault, then syncs it to your Cloud Anchor.

5.2 Permanent Archival

Once all members of a family have completed migration, the legacy Firebase collections for that family are permanently archived and blocked. This action is irreversible. The legacy app will no longer be able to read from or write to those collections.

5.3 Data Integrity

The migration process includes an automated integrity audit. Kypo makes best efforts to ensure completeness but does not guarantee that every legacy record will survive transformation if the original data was corrupt or incomplete.

6. Cloud Anchor & Data Ownership

Your encrypted vault is stored on the cloud provider you select. You own your data. Kypo acts solely as the encryption and synchronisation layer. If you disconnect your Cloud Anchor or delete your cloud storage, Kypo cannot recover your data.

7. Limitation of Liability

To the maximum extent permitted by Swiss law:

  • Kypo is provided “as is” without warranties of any kind.
